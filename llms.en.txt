# Slon.life — Ihor "Slon" Utyuzh, In Memoriam

> Digital memorial of Ihor "Slon" Utyuzh (April 18, 1996 — December 7, 2023) — defender of Ukraine, UAV operator of the 78th Separate Air Assault Regiment, participant of the Revolution of Dignity and the Anti-Terrorist Operation. The site preserves his biography, memories from family, friends and brothers-in-arms, awards, Ukrainian media publications, and the symbols of his path. Created by family and friends in 2024.

The site is a non-commercial memorial and historical-documentary project. All materials are based on testimonies from Ihor's relatives — his parents Oleh and Nina Utyuzh, his fiancée Yevheniia Shkliaruk, and his brothers-in-arms from the 78th Air Assault Regiment.

## Main sections

- [Homepage](https://slon.life/): general overview of the memorial — short biography, key milestones, symbolism of the "Slon" call sign, voices of family and brothers-in-arms.
- [Biography](https://slon.life/biography): full memoir text in five chapters — childhood and character, the Revolution of Dignity and the injury of February 18, 2014, the Anti-Terrorist Operation and the medal "For Sacrifice and Love for Ukraine," return from abroad on February 24, 2022, service in the 78th Air Assault Regiment, final battle on the Zaporizhzhia front.
- [Memories](https://slon.life/memories): voices of those who knew Ihor — family, brothers-in-arms, friends. Visitors can leave their own words of remembrance.
- [Stories](https://slon.life/stories): chronological memorial blog with specific commemoration events — memorial plaque at School No. 277, "Zhadan and the Dogs" concert, 30th birthday tribute, and more.
- [Fragments of Life](https://slon.life/fragments): photo gallery with images of Ihor's life in three thematic sections — "Family" (home, parents, his native Troieshchyna district), "Beloved" (fiancée Yevheniia), "Dreams" (mountains, football, elephants, the "White Elephant" charity foundation). Photographs from the period between Maidan and the front — what he loved, and those he loved.
- [Recognition](https://slon.life/recognition): state and military decorations (Order "For Courage" 3rd class, "Golden Cross," medal "For Sacrifice and Love for Ukraine") and a chronological collection of Ukrainian media publications from 2014, 2023, 2024, and 2025.

## Key facts about the person

- Full name: Utyuzh Ihor Olehovych
- Call sign: "Slon" ("Elephant")
- Date of birth: April 18, 1996, Kyiv
- Date of death: December 7, 2023, near the village of Verbove, Zaporizhzhia Oblast
- Unit: 78th Separate Air Assault Regiment of the Air Assault Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine
- Military specialty: UAV operator
- Education: Kyiv Polytechnic Institute
- Member of the 24th Hundred of the Maidan Self-Defense (2013–2014), wounded on February 18, 2014
- Veteran of the Anti-Terrorist Operation (since 2016)
- Killed in action while shielding his brothers-in-arms from an enemy FPV drone
